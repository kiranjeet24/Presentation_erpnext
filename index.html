Introduction to ERP System

Education Domain

The Education domain in ERP System is designed to meet requirements of any organization which imparts knowledge and believe in doing so in an organized fashion.

Content of Education

Using Education module of ERP System, you can effectively manage operations like:

  1. Managing Student
  2. Program and Courses
  3. Student Attendance
  4. Publishing Programs on the portal
  5. Course Scheduling
  6. Online Admissions
  7. Portal for Publishing Programs
  8. Assessment Planning and Assessment Result
  9. Fee Structure and Fee Receipt
